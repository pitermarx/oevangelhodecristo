Essential Beliefs

About God

  • God exists. He is the creator. He is a trinity
  • God is love. God is holiness
  • God rewards those who seek Him

About Man

  • All men break God's law (sin)
  • Man is separated from God because of sin
  • It is impossible for man to overcome this separation
  • God cannot let humanity's sin go unpunished

About Jesus

  • Jesus is man. Jesus is God. Jesus is the Son of God. And claimed to be equal to God.
  • Jesus was crucified for our sins, enabling our reconciliation with God
  • Jesus resurrected, demonstrating power over death
  • Jesus will return to rule and judge the world

About Our Destiny

  • There are only 2 final destinies for man. With or without God
  • God's forgiveness is free and universal, being applicable only when man:
    1. Recognizes and repents of his sin
    2. Believes in Jesus as Savior
  • A true believer will desire to remove sin from his life. He will do this during his life:
    1. With the help of the Holy Spirit who now dwells in him
    2. With the help of the church that can encourage and teach him
